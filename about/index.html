Our Journey

From a UK startup to New Zealand's trusted insurance comparison platform

2005

Founded in UK

Insurance comparison service established in the United Kingdom with a focus on consumer empowerment.

2010

Expanded to Europe

Successfully expanded operations across multiple European markets, building expertise in diverse insurance landscapes.

2015

First Asian Market

Entered the Asian market, establishing presence in key regional economies and gaining international recognition.

2019

New Zealand Launch

Launched in New Zealand with a commitment to simplifying property insurance for Kiwi homeowners and investors.

2020

COVID-19 Support

Provided critical support to property owners during unprecedented times, helping thousands find coverage solutions.

2021

NZ Compliance

Achieved full compliance with ICNZ standards and became registered with all major NZ insurance regulators.

2022

Product Innovation

Introduced advanced comparison tools and AI-powered quote matching for more accurate recommendations.

2024

Market Leadership

Became the leading independent insurance comparison platform in New Zealand with over 50,000+ active users.

2025

Buildings Insurance Focus

Specialized division launched with expert focus on residential and commercial buildings insurance across New Zealand.
